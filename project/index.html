The Importance Of Addressing Cybercrime.

As technology continues to grow the rates of attack will also increase. Therefore, addressing the malicious techniques before they take action is important to stay safe in the Cyber world. Day to day 560,000 new cyber threats are discovered, this can affect organisations, individuals and threaten global economy. Cybercrime presents detrimental consequences if you have not learnt how to protect yourself. The consequences of Cybercrime have a wide range of impacts from financial losses to identitiy theft.
